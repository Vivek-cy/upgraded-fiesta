Kids are special, they resemble a different sense into us, they make us forget all our burdens, they make us happy. The evolutionary advantage over time that we humans got is mesmerizing.

  1. A baby cannot taste salt until it is 4 months old. The delay may be related to the development of kidneys, which start to process sodium at about that age.
  2. A baby’s eyes are 75% of their adult size, but its vision is around 20/400. By six months, a baby’s vision should reach 20/20.
  3. Newborns are more likely to turn their head to the right than to the left.
  4. Within a few days of birth, a baby can distinguish between the touch of bristles that are of different diameters.
  5. A newborn urinates about every 20 minutes and then roughly every hour at 6 months.
  6. Human babies are the only primates who smile at their parents.
  7. An average baby will go through approximately 2,700 diapers a year.
